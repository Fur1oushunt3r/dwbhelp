There was once a child, Ali, who lived in Syria. He lived a peaceful life until the civil war began...
His world turned uspide down. The war was horrible, his closest friend was shot and killed.
At the same time Ali was dying, he needed a surgery in order to survive, but the health care system in the country had collapsed due to the war.
Luckily, The organisation Doctor Without Borders came to help!
They set up hospitals and started performing surgeries in war zones.
They saved Ali's life and hundred of other children.
